Project 3

Drone Projects

The third project set I am working on is the creation of drones. I have made and programmed several out of the box type drones and ROV's but, now they are parts in my current creations. These includes several track drones, wheel drones and combinations of the two drone types. However on bench currently are:

Aquatic Drone

I am currently working on an aquatic drone that includes 3D printed parts, inductive motors, and multi directional cameras. This creation is fun and unlike anything I have ever done as it is steered by reference to the currently active camera. This switching of cameras and changing of perspective has caused many headaches. Some literally as I swam along side the unit and it changed camera without reason/accident.

Copter Drone

This drone allows a Quad-copter to change configuration and enter an airplane like configuration. Most of this drone is blender model parts burt progress is being made slowly.

At current these are still on hold as I get use to my new 3D printer.
